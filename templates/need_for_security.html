Thus, to prevent hacking and to increase the security of transmission of confidential data, we use encryption methods that converts the data into text that is unreadable at first glance.

These days, large corporations and banks use hashes (eg. MD5, SHA-1) to encrypt their customer's confidential information before sending it to their servers to be validated.

For starters, we have simple encoding methods such as Shift Ciphers which can be easily decoded with simple algorithms. Click here to find out how your encrypted message will look like!
