How does it work?

Image taken from Applied Cryptography Slides Topic 2

  1. Choose how many rails (eg. 2)
  2. Choose a plaintext (eg. HELLOWORLD)
  3. Write the plaintext message as a sequence of diagonals, as seen in the diagram.
  • If we write the plaintext in diagonals,
  • First rail = HLOOL
  • Second rail = ELWRD
  • First rail + Second rail = HLOOLELWRD
